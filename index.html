who we are & what we do

The I T00 AM Critical Makers Lab, a program of the Critical Media Project (CMP), offers an extended learning and enrichment opportunity for 8-11th grade students from South LA schools. The lab is organized around questions related to identity and representation, asking students to reflect on who they are and how they can represent themselves, their community, their story through several media making projects.
